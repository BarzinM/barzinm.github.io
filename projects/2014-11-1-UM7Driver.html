Interests

Here is a short list of my interests

Mechatronics/Robotics

One of the exciting parts of my work is to work with mechatronic components. This can extend from machining support structures, design and manufacturing of moving mechanisms to developing drives for sensors and programming controllers and micro-controllers

Dynamics Modeling and Control System Design

Without knowing this field I cannot make sure that the robotics systems that I work on, specially the ones that have moving parts or electric power systems to behave the way I intend them to do.

Machine Learning

I believe this field can be a solution to many problems in an era that there is more information than what humans can use to full potential.

Image Processing

Improvements in processing capabilities have recently made the way for image processing to perform in real-time. Now we can have more than two eyes.
